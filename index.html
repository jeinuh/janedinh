About Jane

Hi! I'm Jane, a product designer from sunny San Jose, California and a recent grad from UC San Diego. I am interested in how thoughtful design can be used to amplify environmental justice, accessibility, and racial equity.

When I'm not learning new Figma shortcuts, you can find me tending to my numerous houseplants, watching old foreign movies, taking photos of scenic vistas, reading surrealist novels, and/or enjoying a picnic with my dog.
