About Jane

I'm Jane, a senior at UCSD studying design and interaction from the SF Bay Area.
I've been finding joy in design since I was in middle school, where personalizing my blog pages evolved into a hobby for crafting intricate tumblr themes for other users.

Currently, beyond learning as much as I can about formal design principles or new frameworks, I am interested in how thoughtful design can be used to amplify environmental justice, accessibility, and racial equity.

When I'm not finetuning my design skills, you can also find me tending to my numerous houseplants, watching old foreign movies, taking photos of scenic vistas, reading surrealist novels, and/or enjoying a picnic with my dog.
